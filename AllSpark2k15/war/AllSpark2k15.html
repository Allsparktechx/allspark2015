  • April 2015

    Avnet US Hackathon

     Austin, US

    Bluemix with Watson Hackathon. It was an event to explore the Cognitive Computing & Cloud developments in the next frontier of enterprise IT. Mobile Apps were built using Cognitive Computing system of Watson and were showcased along with their Marketing Potential. The cloud development skills were stormed and all the apps were deployed on IBM Bluemix. IBM's Open Cloud Architecture was drilled, leveraging Cloud Foundry to enable developers to rapidly build, deploy, and manage their cloud applications. The event got a worldwide recognition in Avnet, IBM newsletters and other websites. Cash prizes were distributed to the winners.
